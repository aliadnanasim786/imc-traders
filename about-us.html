    ABOUT US

    We are pleased to introduce that Automate Pakistan Private Limited is an “Industrial Automation & Control Company” which emerged in 1990 with an aim to provide best automation solutions to the industry. With over 26 years of experience in the market, Automate is recognized as system integration solutions company with portfolio of diversified products and field services in Pakistan serving all major industrial sectors with the expertise.

    Vision

    To become the leading and preferred by the Industry company in the field of Industrial Automation and Control to meet the client needs by providing customer driven, cost effective and quality solutions.

    Mission

    To exceed customer expectations by offering quality Products, innovative services, product applications, and execution of Electrical Instrumentation and control projects on turnkey basis while staying within the funds allocated for the project To provide our employees with healthy, transparent, enjoyable and rewarding working environment To enhance the value of the firm through profitable joint ventures To serve the society by participating in welfare programs

    Scope of Work

    Supply of industrial instruments to provide solutions for Process Control, machine control, Electrical equipment, Instrumentation, O&M services, industrial control & system integration Supply of Industrial automation and control products like, PLC, DCS, ESD, F&G and SCADA. Expertise of Troubleshooting, Field instrumentation & Installation Services for designing, installation & Commissioning of projects on turnkey basis.
